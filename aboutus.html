The Guide

Yogi

Yogi started walking the rice terraces of Ubud before they were a global destination. Over forty years ago, he realized that what travelers wanted a companion who knew where the river was cleanest and where the coffee was strongest.

He has spent two decades building relationships across the island, from the village heads in the north to the boat captains in the east. If there is a locked gate in Bali, Yogi probably has the key or knows the cousin of the person who does.

The Strategist

Ucok

Growing up in the passenger seat of his father’s car, Ucok saw firsthand how a single bad experience could ruin a holiday. He learned that "Island Time" is beautiful for relaxing, but "Professional Time" is what matters when a guest needs a SIM card or a villa rental fixed now.

Ucok brings the modern energy to All-In Bali. He ensures that while you get an authentic, old-school experience, the logistics, communication, safety, and reliability are world-class.
